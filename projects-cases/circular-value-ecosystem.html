Circular Value Ecosystem Cluster of Higueras, Mexico.

Summary of the project.

Project: Circular Value Ecosystem Cluster of Higueras, Mexico.
Timeline of the Project (diagnostics and CE proposal) May 2014-2016
Next phase (implementation) March 2021---in progress
Project Goal (proposed for the post pandemic) Implementation of the Regional Circular Value System (CVES) model for the creation of sustainable wealth for the town of Higueras Nuevo León, (Mexico) an economic-environmental cluster assembly and a self-sustainable community network, for the creation of an alternative economy for the inhabitants of the town.
Led by Private funding and SWIT Research Group.
Country and Region of implementation of this project/initiative Higueras, Nuevo Leon, Mexico
United Nation SDGs covered in this project/initiative SDG# 8: Decent work and Economic growth. The project aims to offer job opportunities aligned to the preservation of natural resources in the region. By promoting the substitution of unsustainable extractive intensive jobs with economically viable alternatives, productive and decent work is ensured.
SDG# 9: Industry, innovation, and infrastructure. The project promotes sustainable and inclusive industrialization by considering a new economic model for the region that aims to avoid the depletion and contamination of natural resources.
SDG# 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities. The project will result in an alternative economic and social development model for the region that ensures the preservation of natural resources.
The main goal of this project has been to create a new economy for the town, to substitute unsustainable jobs and eliminate the natural deterioration created by a local quarry and other depredators of the natural reserve of Sierra Picacho.
