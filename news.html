SWIT Research Group members among the Semifinalists of WEGE Prize

Two members of SWIT Research Group, Andrea Cantu, and Bernardo Bello, participate in WEGE Prize 2021. They have established a multidisciplinary team together with a student of the Ph.D. in Engineering, Bertha Treviño, and Iqui Heredia from the Bachelor in Mechatronics Engineering, both from Tecnologico de Monterrey, and Israel Zuñiga, an undergraduate mathematics student, UnADM. The team proposed a “Recipe for Leftovers” to solve the problem of food waste with a circular economy approach. They are not semi-finalists of the contest and will move to the second half of the competition. Congratulations and all the best to the team.

SWIT at INCmty 2020 Sustainability Summit

SWIT Research Group Leader Carlos Scheel and Researcher Eduardo Aguiñaga participated at INCmty Sustainability Summit on November 5th, 2020 with a fireside chat session where they debunked the myths that surround the concept of Circular Economy. There were 561 attendants to this hour-long session. Carlos Scheel and Eduardo Aguiñaga participated in the Sustainability Summit of INCmty 2020
