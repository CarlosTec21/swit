Conceptual Axes of SWIT

The SWIT Research Group has identified five main axes or pillars that act as building blocks of the proposed framework: (1) Sustainable Wealth and Prosperity Creation; (2) Circular Value Systems; (3) Systemic Vision and System Based Leadership; (4) Disruptive Systemic and Circular Innovation (DiSC); and (5) Circular SMEs and rural development.
